FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

I'm a sponsor, how can you help my trial?

We offer tools to speed up your trial recruitment. From AI to identify patients while a trial is running, to analyzing our network of providers for site selection and feasability. Email us sponsors@hdphealth.com to find out more.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use encrypted HIPAA compliant databases to keep all of your information safe and secure. We can also remove all of your information at any time should you choose. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

I'm a provider, how can you help?

HDP Health's AI can connect directly to your EMR and identify patients. Our system works also with your doctors and clinical research staff to both improve your recruitment percentages and decrease physician time spent on enrollment. You can learn more on our Providers page.

How does this work as a patient?

You can simply visit our Find a Trial page, type in your condition and other information such as age and location and we'll show you a list of trials we find. You can also have these trials emailed to you.
